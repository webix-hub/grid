Version 11.4 (April 2026)
=============================

### Major features

- Area selection: Ctrl+drag multi-area selection for end users

### Fixes

- Regression: `setState` now restores column state on the first call even when column order differs
- Columns reused from the grid's own config via `define`/`refreshColumns` now render properly
- Multi-area selection borders no longer disappear on the split side when selection crosses a split boundary
- Date-specific filters and editors with Calendar: ISO week numbers are now computed correctly across time zones and DST transitions
- Touch: swipe action on the empty area under data rows could throw an error
- Styling: unified colors of frozen column borders (headers and data)

Version 11.3 (December 2025)
=============================

### Fixes

- Datatable: improved dynamic rendering on touch and hybrid (2-in-1) devices
- Regression: `refreshColumns` should not reapply filtering
- Regression: frequent manual (intentional) `loadNext` calls were blocked
- Custom Scroll on Firefox, Windows: scroll step was too large
- Custom Scroll on Firefox, Windows: custom scroll didn't initialize properly
- Custom Scroll on Firefox, Windows: footer could overlap the horizontal scrollbar
- Custom Scroll on Firefox, Windows: scrollbar areas overlapped the data, preventing selection in the corners
- Enhanced IME support for filters
